    • South Africa

    South Africa offers diverse vacation resorts from Cape Town’s beachside luxury hotels to the exclusive safari lodges in Kruger National Park, as well as the winelands retreats in Stellenbosch.

    Botswana

    Known for its luxury safari lodges in the Okavango Delta and Chobe National Park, Botswana offers exclusive wildlife experiences with high-end accommodations that blend seamlessly with nature.

    Tanzania

    Tanzania is home to world-class resorts on the beaches of Zanzibar and the serene shores of Lake Tanganyika. Safari lodges near the Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater offer breathtaking views and luxurious stays.

    Egypt

    Egypt’s Red Sea resorts, like those in Sharm El Sheikh and Hurghada, provide stunning beach experiences, diving, and historical tours. The Nile cruise ships also offer a unique blend of luxury and ancient history.

    Ethiopia

    Madagascar

    Madagascar’s resorts, particularly on Nosy Be and Île Sainte-Marie, offer pristine beaches, unique wildlife encounters, and luxury eco-lodges that highlight the island’s biodiversity.

    Cape Verde

    This island nation boasts beach resorts on Sal and Boa Vista islands, known for their white sandy beaches, water sports, and laid-back atmosphere with an Afro-Portuguese cultural mix.

    Malawi

    In Malawi, beyond Kaya Mawa and Pumulani Lodge, the country is known for its warm hospitality and stunning natural beauty, particularly around Lake Malawi.
    The lake itself is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known as the "Lake of Stars" due to its clear waters and abundant fish species.

    Rwanda

    Luxury lodges near Volcanoes National Park offer close encounters with mountain gorillas, while Lake Kivu resorts provide stunning lakeside retreats in a peaceful environment.
